Graphic Design

ESTeBank Business Card

OVERVIEW

For this project, I undertook the challenge of designing a professional corporate identity for "ESTeBank," a fictional financial institution. The goal was to establish a sense of security, trustworthiness, and modernity through a cohesive branding package. This required building a robust visual language and ensuring seamless brand continuity from a high-impact digital billboard down to a personal business card for the Branch Manager.

KEY FEATURES

  • Premium Brand Identity: Utilized a sophisticated deep blue and metallic gold color palette to convey authority, luxury, and institutional stability.

  • Figma Workflow: I used Figma for the business card layout, asset positioning, and typography design. Its precise alignment tools and components were essential for maintaining geometric accuracy on the multi-colored background blocks.

  • Photoshop Integration: I leveraged Photoshop for high-end texturing, blending, and lighting. I spent significant time in PS creating realistic, beveled metallic gold gradients and high-impact lighting interactions to elevate the professional look.

WHAT I LEARNED

  • Advanced Cross-Tool Workflow: This project sharpened my ability to bridge Figma and Photoshop efficiently. I learned how to use Figma for layout and typography structure while integrating high-fidelity raster assets created in Photoshop to achieve professional blending and texture effects.

  • Visual Hierarchy in Corporate Branding: I deepened my understanding of using layout to communicate brand values. I prioritized key information flow: first the premium brand logo ("EST Bank"), then the value proposition ("SMART BANKING..."), and finally, the supporting anchors ("Secure • Trusted • Reliable").
