Graphic Design

Burger Promotional Flyer

OVERVIEW

For this project, I took on the challenge of designing a compelling, high-impact marketing flyer for a premium fast-food campaign. The goal was to create a design that didn't just look appetizing but drove immediate action, blending professional commercial photography aesthetics with clear, bold call-to-actions.

KEY FEATURES

  • Composition: I established a strong "dark mode" background utilizing a wooden table texture and subtle spotlight lighting to contrast sharply with the vibrant colors of the burger. This technique emphasizes premium quality.

  • Hierarchical Information Flow: Structured critical data (Price, Offer Type, Discount, Delivery) using logical positioning and typography to ensure readability at a glance.

  • Professional Presentation: Created a secondary mockup of multiple flyers fanned on a neutral background to show the design in a realistic production and marketing context.

WHAT I LEARNED

  • Commercial Psychology and Visual Hierarchy: This project was a major learning experience in arranging marketing elements. I learned how to guide a customer's eye from the central imagery (appealing food) straight to the value proposition ("$45.00 only", "45% off") and then the action ("ORDER NOW").

  • Typography as Texture: I learned how to use textured fonts effectively as graphic elements (the main "BURGER" text), balancing them against clean instructional text (the bottom URL/Address bar) for optimal design balance.
